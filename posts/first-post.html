Starting My Academic Website

May 29, 2026

Welcome to my academic website. I created this space as a central place to share my research interests, technical work, and professional development as a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the Rochester Institute of Technology.

My work sits at the intersection of embedded systems, ambulatory sensing, puff topography monitoring, exposure science, and computational modeling. Through this site, I hope to document the projects, ideas, and methods that shape my research journey.

In addition to presenting my research focus and current work, this website will serve as a place for research notes, technical reflections, and selected updates from my doctoral studies. Over time, I plan to use this blog to write about methods, experiments, lessons learned, and the broader process of developing as a researcher.

Thank you for visiting. I look forward to building this space into a useful record of my academic work and ongoing growth.
