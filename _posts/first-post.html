Welcome to my academic website. I created this space to share my research interests, academic journey, and reflections on the work I am doing in embedded systems, exposure science, and ambulatory sensing.

My goal for this website is to provide a simple and accessible place where visitors can learn more about my background, current research, and evolving scholarly interests. Over time, I hope to use it to document projects, publications, technical writing, and personal reflections from my Ph.D. journey.

As a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the Rochester Institute of Technology, I am especially interested in the use of embedded sensing and data-driven approaches to better understand human behavior and exposure in natural environments.

Thank you for visiting. I look forward to continuing to build and refine this site as my work grows.
