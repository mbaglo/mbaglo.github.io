Welcome

Welcome to my academic website. I am a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the Rochester Institute of Technology and a Graduate Research Assistant in the Respiratory Technologies Laboratory.

My research focuses on embedded sensing, ambulatory monitoring, puff topography, exposure science, behavior-based yield modeling, pharmacokinetic modeling, and data-driven analysis of inhalant product use.
